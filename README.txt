Use Claude, OpenAI, and Ollama AI directly in your Homey Flows.

Ask questions, control smart home devices with natural language, and analyze images — all through simple Flow cards. The AI decides whether to answer your question or control your devices automatically.

Features:
- Ask AI questions from any Flow
- Control devices with natural language ("turn off the living room lights")
- Analyze images with AI (security cameras, deliveries, etc.)
- Three AI providers: Claude (Anthropic), OpenAI, and Ollama (local/private)
- Provider fallback: if one provider fails, automatically try the next
- Conversation memory: multi-turn conversations across Flows
- Webhook API for external integrations
